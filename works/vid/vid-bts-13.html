Video Practicum – Behind the Scenes

2013
Category: Video & Animation
Role: Filmed, Directed, Edited
Used: Canon Rebel t4i, Premiere Pro

A short documentary-style behind-the-scenes film of what a college video production practicum class is like. Students discuss their experience in the class and reflect upon the difficulties of group work.

I filmed and directed the video as well as edited it together. The original version was about 8 minutes long, and this is a re-edited version that fits my portfolio better.

The students in the group were: Simon Alfrey, Alex Amos, Hannah Carbaugh, Tabitha Kennedy, Jake Pedzinski, and Kevin Petrowsky. Other individuals who were featured include: Alice Guest-Mckellar, Kevin Petrowsky, Nathan McDaniel.
