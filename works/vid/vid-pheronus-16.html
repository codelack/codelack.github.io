A retired superhero struggles with ignoring normal crimes of everyday urban life after appeasing the villains of New York City.

This was my first independently-produced short film made outside of school. It was co-produced and sound recorded by Tim Kennedy II and stars Jake Beyler and Tierra Sims.

This film coincides with my artistic themes of weird, sci-fi, and real-life depictions of superheroes, as well as mental disorders (in this case, depression).
