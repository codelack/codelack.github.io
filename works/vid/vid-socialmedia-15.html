I created an animation in After Effects cs6 to promote IU Kokomo’s social media for an internship with the Office of Media and Marketing. The video was used on the campus’ digital signs for a semester.

I utilized the school’s branding guidelines for this project and edited the given icons in Illustrator to match the composition.

I used footage of campus flowers filmed by Mike Glassburn as the background video.
