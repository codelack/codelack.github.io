IU Kokomo Hero Videos

2015
Category: Video & Animation
Role: Footage, Editing
Used: Premiere Pro

I edited together video of IU Kokomo’s campus to create temporary, moving “hero” videos for the school’s homepage. The first hero is shown playing live on the homepage in the third video shown.

I did this as a part of an internship with the Office of Media and Marketing at IU Kokomo.

While I edited the videos, I took some of the footage. Other footage was captured by my supervisor, Mike Glassburn.
