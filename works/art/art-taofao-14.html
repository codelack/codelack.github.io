Tab Kennedy

Dragon in TAOFAO

2014
Category: Fine Arts
Genre: Digital Art
Media: FireAlpaca, MyPaint, Photoshop, WACOM Bamboo

I drew this piece from start to finish with a WACOM Bamboo Pen. I started off with the base sketch and colors in FireAlpaca, did the modeling in MyPaint, and finished it off by adjusting colors and textures in Photoshop.

The characters in this drawing belong to a fictional story called The Adventures of Flufferson and Oliver, or TAOFAO for short, which I co-own with a friend. The atmosphere of their adventures are comedic and cartoony.
