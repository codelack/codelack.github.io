Tab Kennedy

Teeth

2013
Category: Fine Arts
Genre: Digital Art
Media: MyPaint, WACOM Bamboo

I drew this piece for the first publication of the art and literary magazine, Jar of Teeth, published by SGD Publishing. The August 2013 issue includes 42 artists and 6 writers. The first issue’s themes were “teeth” and “yellow.”
