DOTS, or “Depending on the Situation,” represents the graphic design and music I make in my free time. I have made a very simple, small website including some of my “album” covers.

To proceed coding another website in plain code, I was instructed to show that I could use Dreamweaver first. This is a simple 3-page website I designed and coded for the class “introduction to new media communication.” The majority of this website was coded within one night.
