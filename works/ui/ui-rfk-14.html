Proceed to this work

This is a website designed and coded as an alternative to the current website for IU Kokomo’s Radio Free Kokomo. I contributed heavily to the evaluating, re-designing, and coding. I worked in Adobe Illustrator for the designing and GitHub’s Atom for the coding.

I worked in a team to produce this for the class “website design and development.”

Contributors include: Rob Trlak, Tabitha Kennedy, Theral Jessop, Emily Herrington, Sean Conrad.
