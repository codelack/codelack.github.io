I planned and designed a touchscreen wayfinder kiosk for IU Kokomo’s Welcome Center as a part of a Gerald L. Bepko internship on IU Kokomo’s web team.

The system includes three basic functionalities: Find People, Find Places, and Accessibility. People can find out more about faculty and staff contact information and office locations, they can interact with a map to find where they would like to go on campus, and they can see where ramps, accessible parking, and the accessibility office are.

I also created a moving screensaver in After Effects, which plays in a loop until someone touches the screen.

I worked under the supervision of Ed Ventura and Kathy Kennedy. After the completion of the design, the system was coded by Abby Burkhalter.
